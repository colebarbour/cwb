About

Cole Barbour is an award-winning sound engineer and experienced video producer located in Boston, MA. He creates a wide variety of high quality audio and video products ranging from album recordings to concert livestreamings. He has a Bachelor in music and a Master in sound recording from McGill University, Montreal, Canada. Mr. Barbour works as an assistant audio engineer for the Boston Symphony Orchestra and is an active video producer in Boston, working with clients such as the Harvard Music Association and the Boston Artists Ensemble.
